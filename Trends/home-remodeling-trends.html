Home Remodeling Trends for the New Year


In 2023, turning the page on a new year means making a bold move with the design of the home. Stepping into the spotlight and out of a comfort zone requires vision and planning that allows a homeowner to put their best foot forward in the great unknown of today.

With an expansive product catalog and the professional expertise to blend home improvement trends with the daring looks of today, ProSource Wholesale® is the source to convert remodeling dreams into reality.

Below is a handful of home remodeling trends that warrant consideration in 2023.






Arches and Curves When making a bold move in a room design, something that draws the eye may be necessary, in order to embody the support and strength that it takes to make such a change. Symbolizing that strength is the archway, which can accentuate the oasis of the bathtub or center the family around the hearth of the fireplace. It also offers support above doorways and windows, as well as aesthetic support in mirrors, wallpaper, and other wall art.

Curvature is being built into other mainstays of the home. Arch-style kitchen cabinets feature curved edges, while circular shelves within the storage accentuate the style of 2023. Curved kitchen islands, bars, and seating allow for more entertaining. In living areas, furniture pieces like the accent chair and sofa are beginning to curve, bringing families closer during movie or game night.





A Transition to Maximalism Bid farewell to the days when less was more. Today’s modern home makes daring design choices with their décor. Stunning options like rich fabrics and luxurious textures are only met with the poignant layered patterning and deep, jewel tones that maximalism offers.

Bringing the room together are warm brass and gold accent pieces. In living rooms, look to mirrors and wall art. In a kitchen, such hardware can make the cabinetry pop, replacing monochromatic black. This is making rooms flourish alongside gold-accented wallpaper and elegant wall sconces that boast a playful prominence in in 2023.





The Evolving Laundry Room Gone are the perceptions of the dark, derelict laundry room found deep in the trenches of an unfinished basement. In 2023, this cleaning space is in the symphony of daring style choices, giving new life to this area of the home.

Decorated with design-centric backsplashes and artisanal floating shelves, the laundry room has been rethought of as a space to do more than wash and hang clothing. It has evolved into a room to admire, similar to other areas of the home. Creating that space requires the boldness that 2023 provides. With durable and decorative floor choices meeting the bold décor along the walls, the laundry room has been reclaimed by the home.





Home Is Where the Hard Work Is The pandemic has taught millions around the world how to use the home for a variety of purposes. One of the most practical purposes, utilizing an area for work purposes, ventures on as one of the most trendy. Home offices provide a blank canvas to express personal style and create a fashion-forward functional work environment.

Highlight those walls with Zoom-friendly wall art and shelves, and adorn the room with ergonomic furnishings. The new reality is those virtual meetings become more comfortable.





Carpet in the Bedroom If the trendy move of 2023 is letting décor of the home pop, then carpet is the choice trend for making bedrooms embrace this movement. Never substituting comfort for style, carpet fosters an aura of tranquility and oasis with its naturally restorative textures and fibers.

The feelings emanated from carpet creates a aura of retreat. With its palette of organic tones, warm neutrals, pastel greens, and golden shades, homeowners can set out for that perfect night of sleep and relaxation.
